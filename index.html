AITM College Website

Backend Developer

The AITM College Website project involved creating a comprehensive and user-friendly online platform for the college. The website was designed to provide essential information to students, faculty, and visitors, including academic programs, campus facilities, and important announcements.

One of the key features of the website is the interactive course catalog, which allows students to browse through various courses offered by the college. Each course listing includes detailed descriptions, prerequisites, and contact information for the course instructors.

The website also includes a dedicated section for campus life, highlighting student activities, clubs, and events. This section aims to foster a sense of community among students and encourage participation in extracurricular activities.

To ensure accessibility, the website was designed to be responsive and compatible with various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. Additionally, the website incorporates SEO best practices to improve its visibility on search engines.
