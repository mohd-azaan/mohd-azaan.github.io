AITM College Website

INTERACTIVE INSTALLATION

CREATIVE DIRECTOR

At CES 2024, we played a pivotal role in showcasing Google's Android mobile operating system by creating a unique 18,000-square-foot interactive space.

This space was highlighted by an impressive 80-foot canopy and featured 15 analog-inspired interactive stations, designed to allow attendees to explore Android's latest features in an environment that reflected Google's open and connected philosophy.

Moving away from Google's typical minimalist presence, we adopted a more engaging "choose your own adventure" style, transforming the exhibit into a modern-day "Visitor Attraction Center."

Here, attendees could engage with the technologies that interested them the most. Additionally, we implemented skeuomorphic design elements to make the innovative features approachable, exemplified by a postcard-making station modeled after a mail collection box, which helped make the technology both accessible and familiar to users.
